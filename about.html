Our History

The Beginning

At Cawfee, our journey began with a simple passion for coffee and a dream to create a welcoming space for coffee lovers in our community. Founded in 2023, we started as a small, cozy café with just a handful of coffee beans and a vision. Our founder, Ohio Boss, was inspired by the rich flavors and aromas of coffee encountered during travels to Tilted Towers, leading to the idea of bringing a unique coffee experience back home.

Commitment to Quality

In the early days, we focused on sourcing the finest beans from sustainable farms, ensuring that each cup served at Cawfee not only tasted great but also contributed positively to the environment. Our commitment to quality quickly resonated with our customers, and word about our exceptional brews began to spread.

Looking Ahead

Today, we continue to uphold our founding values, focusing on quality, community, and sustainability. As we look to the future, we’re excited to explore new coffee trends, expand our reach, and deepen our relationships within the community. Thank you for being a part of our journey—we invite you to sit back, relax, and enjoy a cup of coffee with us!

Mission

Our mission at Cawfee is to create a warm and inviting space where every cup of coffee brings people together. We are committed to sourcing high-quality beans, supporting sustainable practices, and crafting each drink with care and passion. With every brew, we aim to foster community connections, spread joy, and offer a moment of tranquility in the bustle of daily life. We believe in the power of coffee to unite, inspire, and create lasting memories, one cup at a time.
