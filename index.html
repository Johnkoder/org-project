About Us

At Cawfee, we believe that great coffee is about more than just taste—it’s about creating a space where everyone feels at home. Located in the heart of 123 Skibidi Street, our shop is a place where community and flavor come together.

We carefully source our beans from sustainable farms and work with skilled roasters to bring out the rich, complex notes in every cup. Each brew is handcrafted by our baristas, who are as passionate about quality as they are about creating a certified rizz approved experience.

What we Offer

Different kinds of coffee

Our coffee is made from the finest, sustainably sourced beans, roasted to perfection and brewed with care. From classic espressos to specialty lattes, each cup is crafted to bring out rich, full flavors.

Handcrafted Pastries & Treats

Pair your coffee with our fresh, locally baked pastries, from flaky croissants to indulgent brownies. We offer a rotating selection of sweet and savory treats to satisfy every craving.

Delivery Service

Get your favorite coffee and treats delivered right to your door! Whether you’re at home or the office, enjoy the same quality and freshness with our reliable delivery service, available through popular delivery apps.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of coffee do you offer?
We offer a wide selection of coffee drinks, from classic espresso and lattes to specialty seasonal blends and cold brews. Whether you’re in the mood for a bold espresso shot or a smooth, creamy latte, we’ve got something for every taste!
Do you have non-dairy milk options?
Absolutely! We offer a variety of non-dairy options, including almond, oat, and soy milk. Just let us know your preference, and we’ll make your drink just the way you like it.
Is there free Wi-Fi available?
Yes, we offer free Wi-Fi for all our customers. Whether you're working remotely or just browsing while you sip, feel free to connect and enjoy our cozy space.
Can I bring my own cup?
Yes, we encourage you to bring your own cup! Not only do you save on waste, but we also offer a small discount to our customers who choose reusable cups. It’s our way of supporting eco-friendly habits!
